# Canva research

Checked: 2026-07-13

## Current strengths

### Verified — official

- Canva's Visual Suite connects documents, email, sheets, whiteboards, presentations, social posts, video, websites, and print in one environment.
- It already offers real-time collaboration, comments and tasks, templates, media libraries, brand controls, animation, resizing, captions, and editable imports.
- Brand Kit centralizes fonts, colors, logos, and brand rules.
- Canva supports conversational and manual creative workflows, as well as editable AI-generated layouts through features such as Magic Layers.

### Announced

Canva AI 2.0 is described on Canva's current page as “Coming Soon.” Announced capabilities include:

- creating by text or voice;
- using connected-tool context;
- conversational refinement followed by manual editing;
- generating images, charts, and copy;
- applying brand rules;
- performing web research;
- learning style over time;
- scheduling recurring work.

These should be treated as announced product direction, not proof that every capability is generally available today.

## What Canva has already commoditized

- Multi-format creation.
- Template discovery.
- Drag-and-drop editing.
- Brand kits.
- Resize and repurpose workflows.
- Conversational creative generation.
- Large asset libraries and collaboration.

Trying to beat Canva on general editing breadth in one week is not credible.

## Differentiation to pursue

1. **Evidence before canvas:** the source set and claim state are first-class.
2. **Claim-level provenance:** a user can inspect why a sentence, number, or scene exists.
3. **Cross-asset dependency graph:** change one approved fact or design rule and see every affected output.
4. **Portable creative constitution:** `SOURCES.json`, `FRAME.md`, and `DESIGN.md` are visible and exportable.
5. **Model and tool flexibility:** OpenAI models, connectors, and deterministic renderers can be selected per job without fragmenting the project.
6. **Voice-directed revision:** “Shorten the proof section and update the deck and video” is a single grounded operation.

## Product boundary

For the hackathon, provide structured editing rather than a blank design canvas:

- reorder or hide sections/scenes;
- choose from approved layouts;
- edit text with source-state warnings;
- replace imagery;
- adjust density, aspect ratio, and tone;
- regenerate one component;
- propagate an approved change to dependent assets.

This is enough to demonstrate creative control without building Canva.
