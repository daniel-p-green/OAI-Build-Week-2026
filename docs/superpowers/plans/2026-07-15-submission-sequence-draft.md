## Phase 3 — Recording loop

**Goal:** produce a demo film that proves all four judging criteria in under three minutes.

Repeat this cycle until the film is ready:

- [ ] Reset the fixture, rehearse once, and record the complete path from the intended in-app surface.
- [ ] Watch the result as a judge and write a concrete critique list: stumbles, labels needing narration, and dead seconds.
- [ ] Burn down the critique list while keeping acceptance green at every merge.
- [ ] Re-record.

Every candidate film must:

- [ ] Show live GPT-5.6 on screen rather than only claim it.
- [ ] Show both approval gates, one edit that demonstrates control, and one plugin-widget doorway moment.
- [ ] Trace an output block to a source sentence.
- [ ] Reveal the brainstorm-to-finished-submission provenance.
- [ ] Match Codex and GPT-5.6 disclosures exactly to the evidence and visibly disclose the AI voice.
- [ ] Target 2:40–2:45 with a 2:55 ceiling; use clear audio, remove dead time, and keep Google marks out of the title, thumbnail, and UI.

Also capture portfolio ephemera during recording: session views, intermediate states, and director's-cut footage.

**Exit:** a take ready to show judges without qualification.
