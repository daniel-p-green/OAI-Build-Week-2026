## Phase 3 — Recording loop

**Goal:** Produce a judge-ready demo under three minutes.

For every cycle:

- [ ] Reset the fixture, rehearse once, and record from the intended in-app surface.
- [ ] Watch the take as a judge and record every stumble, unclear label, and dead second.
- [ ] Fix the critique list while keeping acceptance green.
- [ ] Record again.

Every candidate film must show:

- [ ] Real GPT-5.6 behavior on screen, described no more strongly than the evidence supports.
- [ ] Both approvals, one edit that demonstrates control, and one plugin doorway moment.
- [ ] A source trace from an Output claim to an exact source excerpt.
- [ ] The brainstorm-to-finished-submission reveal.
- [ ] Accurate Codex, GPT-5.6, and AI-voice disclosures.
- [ ] A target runtime of 2:40–2:45 and a ceiling of 2:55, with clear audio, no dead time, and no Google marks in the title, thumbnail, or UI.

Also preserve useful session views, intermediate states, and director's-cut footage.

**Exit:** A take that can be shown to judges without apology or explanatory repair.
